Diminutives
I have a blog. Despite the fact that not many people read it and I post infrequently, I'm very concerned that it be "correct". I want arguments to be airtight, the prose free of typos, and the styling decent.

The purpose of this space it to write things more freely. I want to try out ideas; it's possible I'll change my mind on them, and I hope to be forgiven.

The styling is ~10 lines of CSS. There will definitely be typos.

The work 'diminutive' indicates smaller in size and less formal. Perfect!
