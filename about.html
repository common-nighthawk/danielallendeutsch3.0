About

Daniel is a web developer that lives in Brooklyn, New York. He passionately loves clean and clever code.

He prefers burritos to tacos, and wishes he watched a little less television. For reasons unknown, he has strong affinities for the Oxford comma and double sentence spacing. He gets slightly maniacal when talking about Vim.

In chronological order he--

  • grew up in northern New Jersey
  • graduated from the University of Pennsylvania
  • spent a year traveling and working across South America, the Middle East, and Europe
  • worked at Time Inc. doing retention marketing (think direct mail, pricing strategy, P&L management)
  • worked at Barnes & Noble/NOOK as a marketing analyst (think marketing campaigns, data analysis, growing a young business)
  • works at MAP Digital as a developer, extending all levels of the stack of a complex web application as part of a small team
  • is currently reading these books: 'Professional JavaScript for Web Developers', 'The Lean Startup', 'Hacking: The Art of Exploitation'
